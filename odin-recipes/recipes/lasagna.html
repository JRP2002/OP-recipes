Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

It is perfect for family dinners and can be prepared ahead of time for convenience.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce in a pan.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly.
