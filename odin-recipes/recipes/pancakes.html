Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a simple and delicious breakfast option, fluffy and golden brown.

They are perfect with syrup, fruit, or butter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and eggs and whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden.
